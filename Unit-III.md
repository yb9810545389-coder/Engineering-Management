### **3.1 Motivation (Prerana)**

**Definition:**
Motivation vaneko tyo **"Inner Drive"** (vitri shakti) ho jasle employee lai company ko goal achieve garna mehenat garna uksauxa. "To Move" vanne Latin word bata aako ho.

#### **3.1.1 Theories of Motivation (Most Important for Viva)**

Yaha 4 wota main theory chhan. Viva ma external le "Kunai euta explain gar" vanna sakchha.

**1. Maslow’s Hierarchy of Needs (The Pyramid):**
Abraham Maslow le manchhe ko needs lai 5 level ma baneko chha. Tala ko need pura navayi manchhe mathi jadaina.
*   **Level 1: Physiological Needs (Basic):** Gaas, Baas, Kapaas (Food, Shelter). Paila pet varinu paryo.
*   **Level 2: Safety Needs:** Job security, Health insurance. "Bholi k khane?" vanne dar huna vayena.
*   **Level 3: Social Needs:** Maya, Sathi-bhai, Belongingness. Office ma sathi chaincha.
*   **Level 4: Esteem Needs:** Ijjat, Respect, Job Title. "Ma falano post ma chu" vanne ghamanda.
*   **Level 5: Self-Actualization:** Life ko purpose, Creativity. "Maile kehi thulo gare" vanne feel hune.

**2. Herzberg’s Two-Factor Theory (Hygiene vs. Motivators):**
Yesle vanchha, manchhe lai duita kura le asar garchha:
*   **Hygiene Factors (Maintenance):** Yiniharu **Dissatisfaction huna bata rokchha**, tara motivate gardaina.
    *   *Example:* Salary, AC, Ramro कुर्सी, Company Policy. (Yo vayena vane manchhe risauxa, tara vayo vandai ma makhha parera dherai kaam gardaina, neutral bascha).
*   **Motivators (Growth):** Yiniharu le chai **Real Motivation** dincha.
    *   *Example:* Achievement, Recognition (Tareef), Responsibility, Growth opportunities.

**3. Expectancy Theory (Vroom):**
Manchhe le kaam garnu aghi 3 wota kura calculation garchha:
1.  **Expectancy (Effort $\to$ Performance):** "Maile mehenat gare vane result aauxa ta?"
2.  **Instrumentality (Performance $\to$ Reward):** "Result aayo vane malai reward milcha ta?"
3.  **Valence (Reward Value):** "Tyo reward malai man parcha ta?"
*   *Conclusion:* Yadi yiniharu positive chhan vane matra manchhe le kaam garchha.

**4. Equity Theory (Adams):**
Yo **"Fairness"** (Nishpakshata) ma aadharit chha.
*   Employee le sadhai aafno **Input** (Mehenat, Time) ra **Output** (Salary, Bonus) lai aru sathi (Reference) sanga daajchha.
*   Yadi maile sathi jati nai kaam gare, tara usle badi paisa payo vane ma **Demotivate** hunchu.

---

#### **3.1.2 Techniques for Motivation (Kasari Motivate garne?)**

*   **Financial Incentives:** Bonus, Salary hike, Profit sharing.
*   **Non-Financial Incentives:**
    *   **Job Rotation:** Euta kaam bata bor vayo vane arko ma pathaune.
    *   **Participation:** Decision lida employee lai sodhne.
    *   **Recognition:** "Employee of the Month" dine, praise garne.
    *   **Flexible Working Hours:** Aafno time ma kaam garna dine (Work from home).

### **3.2 Leadership (Netritwa)**

**Definition:**
Leadership vaneko **"Process of Influencing others"** ho. Manager le power use garchha, Leader le influence use garchha.

#### **3.2.1 Leadership Styles (Viva Favorite)**

**1. Autocratic (Tanashahi):**
*   *Concept:* "Maile je vaney tei gar." Leader le eklai decision lincha.
*   *Use:* Crisis ko bela, Military ma, ya unskilled worker vako thau ma thik huncha.
*   *Drawback:* Creative team (Engineers) lai yo man pardaina.

**2. Democratic (Participative):**
*   *Concept:* "Timiharu k vanchau? Sallah garau." Team member sanga discuss garera decision line.
*   *Use:* Skilled manpower (IT companies) ma best huncha.
*   *Drawback:* Decision lina time lagna sakchha.

**3. Servant Leadership (Sewak):**
*   *Concept:* "Ma timiharuko boss haina, helper hu." Leader le aafu lai team ko sewak thancha. Usle team ko badha-adchan (obstacles) hatauxa.
*   *Use:* **Agile/Scrum** projects ma Scrum Master vaneko Servant Leader ho.

**4. Transformational (Pariwartankari):**
*   *Concept:* Yesle company ko vision nai change garchha. Inspire garera manchhe bata best nikalcha.
*   *Example:* Steve Jobs, Elon Musk.

---

#### **3.2.2 Characteristics of Learning Organization in ICT**

**Concept:**
IT/ICT field sadhai change vairakhcha (aaja ko tech bholi purano huncha). Tei vayera organization le pani sadhai sikirakhnu parcha. **Peter Senge** le yesko concept layeko ho.

**Characteristics:**
1.  **Systems Thinking:** Sabai kura connected chha vanera bujhne.
2.  **Personal Mastery:** Harek employee le aafno skill (coding/design) ma expert hunu parcha.
3.  **Mental Models:** Purano sochai tyagera naya tarika le sochnu parcha.
4.  **Shared Vision:** Sabai jana euta common goal ko lagi lagne.
5.  **Team Learning:** Ek-arkalai sikaune culture (Knowledge Sharing).

---

### **3.3 Challenges and Strategies for Technical Workforce**

Engineers/Technical manchhe lai manage garna garo huncha kina ki uniharu logical hunchan ra micromanagement man paraudainan.

**Challenges (Samasya):**
*   **Burnout:** Dherai pressure ra deadline le garda chado thakchan.
*   **Rapid Tech Change:** Naya technology sikirakhnu parne tanab.
*   **Lack of Soft Skills:** Engineer haru technical ma talented vayeni communication ma weak huna sakchan.
*   **Remote Work Issues:** Team members world ko farak farak thau ma huda coordination garne garo.

**Strategies (Samadhan):**
*   **Autonomy:** Uniharu lai kasari kaam garne vanera nasikaune, "K outcome chaincha" tyo matravanne. (Don't Micromanage).
*   **Continuous Learning:** Training ra Certification ko lagi budget dine.
*   **Clear Career Path:** "Timi aaja Developer chau, bholi Architect banchau" vanera future dekhaune.
*   **Recognition:** Technical achievement lai acknowledge garne.
