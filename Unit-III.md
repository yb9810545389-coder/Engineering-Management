# Unit III: Project Management and Planning

## Topics
1. Introduction to Project Management  
2. Project Life Cycle  
   - Initiating  
   - Planning  
   - Executing  
   - Monitoring and Controlling  
   - Closing  
3. Project Scope Management  
4. Time Management  
5. Cost Management  
6. Quality Management  
7. Risk Management  
8. Stakeholder Management  
9. Agile Project Management  
10. Tools and Techniques for Project Management  

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the fundamental principles of project management.
- Learn how to define project scope and objectives.
- Acquire skills in project scheduling and budgeting.
- Understand how to manage risks and stakeholder expectations.
- Explore the various project management methodologies and tools.
