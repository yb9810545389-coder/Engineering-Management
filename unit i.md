### **Unit I: Introduction (6 Hrs.)**

**Core Concept:**
Sir/Mam, Engineering padhera hami technically ta strong hunchau (Code garna, design garna aauxa), tara real world ma **Project** ra **Company** chalauna technical skill le matrapugdaina. Tyo technical knowledge lai **Business Goal** sanga jodna **Management** chaincha. Unit 1 le tei "Bridge" ko kaam garcha.

---

### **1.1 Management (Byabasthapan)**

**1. Definition:**
Simple bhasa ma vanda, **"Management is the art of getting things done through people."**
Manager le aafai itta bokne haina, usle team lai bokna lagaune ho, sahi tarika le.
*   **Efficiency:** Kaam lai *Sasto* ra *Chito* garne (Doing things right).
*   **Effectiveness:** *Sahi* kaam garne (Doing the right things).
*   *Neplish Logic:* "Thorai resource ma dherai output nikalnu nai Management ho."

**2. Functions of Management (Most Important - POSDCORB):**
Manager le dinvari k garcha ta?
1.  **Planning (Yojana):** "Bholi k garne?" aaja nai decide garne. Goal set garne.
2.  **Organizing (Sangathan):** Plan pura garna k k chaincha? (Laptop, Manchhe, Paisa). Tyo jutaune ra structure banaune.
3.  **Staffing (Bhartaa):** Right manchhe lai Right thau ma rakhne (Hiring).
4.  **Leading/Directing (Nirdeshan):** Team lai "Lau hai keta ho, garna parcha" vanera motivate garne, guide garne.
5.  **Controlling (Niyantran):** Plan anusar kaam vayo ki vayena check garne. Vayena vane sachaune.

**3. Levels of Management (Hierarchy):**
Company ma sabai manager eutai hudainan.
*   **Top Level (Strategic):**
    *   *Who:* CEO, CTO, Board of Directors.
    *   *Work:* Long-term vision banaune (5-10 years). "Hamro company lai Nepal ko No. 1 IT company banaune."
    *   *Skill:* Conceptual skill chaincha (Sochne shakti).
*   **Middle Level (Tactical):**
    *   *Who:* Department Heads (Marketing Manager, HR Manager).
    *   *Work:* Top level ko vision lai Department ko plan ma convert garne. Resources allocate garne.
    *   *Skill:* Human skill chaincha (Manchhe milaune).
*   **Lower Level (Operational):**
    *   *Who:* Supervisors, Team Leads, Foremen.
    *   *Work:* Daily kaam garaune. Staff ko attendance herne, quality check garne.
    *   *Skill:* Technical skill chaincha (Kaam kasari garne thaha huna parcha).

### **1.2 Organization (Sangathan)**

**Definition:**
Euta common goal ko lagi dherai manchhe milera kaam garne thau/samuha nai Organization ho.

**Types of Organization (Viva Question!):**

1.  **Formal Organization:**
    *   **Concept:** Management le banako official structure.
    *   **Features:** Clear rules huncha, Hierarchy huncha (Boss $\to$ Subordinate).
    *   **Example:** Tapai ko College, Bank, Thulo IT Company.
    *   *Faida:* Discipline huncha, Zimmedari clear huncha.
    *   *Befaida:* Slow decision making (Red tape).

2.  **Informal Organization:**
    *   **Concept:** Manchhe ko social nature le aafai banne group.
    *   **Features:** Kunai likhit rule hudaina. Friendship ra interest ko aadhar ma bancha.
    *   **Example:** Office ko "Chiya guff group", "Smoking zone group".
    *   *Faida:* Communication chito huncha, Social need pura huncha.
    *   *Befaida:* Rumors (Halla) failincha, management ko birodh garna sakcha.

3.  **Virtual Organization (Very Imp for ICT):**
    *   **Concept:** Physical office nahuna pani sakcha. Internet (ICT) use garera world ko jun sukai kuna bata kaam garne.
    *   **Features:** Flexible huncha, low cost (office rent bacchha), Outsourcing ma chalcha.
    *   **Example:** Remote working teams, Freelancing groups.
    *   *Challenge:* Communication gap huna sakcha, Trust issue huna sakcha.

### **1.3 Engineering Management**

**1. Definition:**
Engineering Management vaneko **"Engineering principles"** ra **"Management skills"** ko mixture ho.
*   Euta Engineer le *Technical* kura bujhcha (Code, Circuit).
*   Euta Manager le *Business* kura bujhcha (Profit, Deadline).
*   **Engineering Manager** le duwai bujhcha ra **Technical Team** lai lead garcha.

**2. Importance in Technology-driven Environment:**
Aaja ko jamana ma technology change vairakhcha. Only technical vayera pugdaina:
*   **Bridge Gap:** Technical team ra Non-technical client bich kura bujhauna.
*   **Innovation:** Naya technology lai business ma kasari use garne vanera sochna.
*   **Efficiency:** Mahango engineering resource (Developers) lai sahi tarika le use garna.

**3. Engineering Functions in Organization:**
Euta Engineering company ma k k kaam huncha?
*   **Product Development:** Naya software/hardware banaune (R&D).
*   **Operations:** Baneko system lai daily chalaune (Maintenance, Server uptime).
*   **Quality Assurance (QA):** Product ma bug chha ki chaina check garne.
*   **IT Systems:** Internal network, security, ra tools manage garne.

**4. Roles & Responsibilities of Engineering Manager:**
External le sodhyo vane, yasto vandinus:
*   **People Management:** Hiring, firing, training, motivating engineers.
*   **Project Management:** Deadline vitra project sakne, budget control garne.
*   **Technical Leadership:** Team lai technical direction dine (Architecture decide garne).
*   **Communication:** Upper management ra team bich ko pul (bridge) banne.
