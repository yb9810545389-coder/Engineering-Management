# Unit IV: Communication and Stakeholder Management

## Topics
1. **Introduction to Communication**  
   - Definition and Importance  
   - Types of Communication  
   - Communication Models  

2. **Stakeholder Management**  
   - Identifying Stakeholders  
   - Analyzing Stakeholder Needs  
   - Engaging Stakeholders

3. **Effective Communication Strategies**  
   - Verbal and Non-Verbal Communication  
   - Active Listening  
   - Feedback Mechanisms  

4. **Conflict Resolution**  
   - Understanding Conflict  
   - Techniques for Resolution  
   - Negotiation Skills

5. **Communication Tools and Technologies**  
   - Digital Communication Tools  
   - Collaboration Platforms  
   - Project Management Software

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the fundamental principles of communication and its impact on project success.  
- Identify and analyze key stakeholders for effective stakeholder management.  
- Develop and apply effective communication strategies to enhance collaboration and engagement.  
- Demonstrate skills in conflict resolution and negotiation within stakeholder interactions.  
- Utilize modern tools and technologies to facilitate communication and project management.
