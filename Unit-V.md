### **5.1 Emerging Management Concepts**

**1. Participative Management (Sahabhagita-mulak):**
*   **Concept:** Decision lida boss le eklai naline, team members (Engineers) lai pani sodhne.
*   **Kina Chaincha?** Engineer haru creative hunchan. Uniharu sanga solution huncha. Uniharule decision ma vag lina paye vane **"Yo project mero pani ho"** vanne ownership feel garchan (Engagement badhcha).

**2. Conflict Resolution (Jhagada Milaune):**
Team ma different opinion hunu normal ho. Tara tesle kaam roknu vayena.
*   **Types:**
    *   **Technical Conflict:** Design A ramro ki B? (Yo ramro ho, yesle solution nikalcha).
    *   **Personal Conflict:** "Tyo manchhe malai man pardaina." (Yo naramro ho).
*   **Resolution Styles (Viva Q):**
    *   **Collaboration (Win-Win):** Sabai ko kura sunera best solution nikalne. (Engineering ma Best).
    *   **Compromise:** Ali ali duibai le tyagne.
    *   **Avoidance:** Ignore garne (Sano kura ma).

**3. Change Management (Pariwartan):**
Company ma naya technology (e.g., Waterfall bata Agile ma shift huda) lyaunda employee haru daraauxan (**Resistance**).
*   **Challenge:** "Hami lai purano nai thik thiyo, naya kina sikne?"
*   **Solution:** Training dine, faida bujhaune, ra process lai smooth banaune.

**4. Quality Management (TQM):**
Quality vaneko Testing Department ko matra kaam haina.
*   **Total Quality Management (TQM):** Company ko **Harek Manchhe** (Receptionist dekhi CEO samma) quality improve garna lagnu parcha.
*   **Six Sigma:** Defects (galti) lai zero ko najik puryaune technique.

**5. Innovation and Disruption (Naya Soch):**
*   **Innovation:** Purano kura lai improve garne.
*   **Disruptive Innovation (Viva Favorite):** Yesto naya idea jasle purano market lai purai khattam pardincha.
    *   *Example:* **Netflix** le Video Rental store (Blockbuster) lai disrupt garyo. **Digital Camera** le Kodak lai disrupt garyo. **Uber/Pathao** le Taxi system lai disrupt garyo.

### **5.2 Recent Engineering Management Concepts (ICT Focus)**

Yo topic bata external le "Timi haru IT/Computer ko student hau, l lau vana ta..." vanera sodhchha.

**1. Agile Methodologies:**
*   **Concept:** Purano (Waterfall) model ma pura plan banayera matra kaam suru hunthyo. Agile ma hami **Sano Sano Tukra (Sprints)** ma kaam garchau.
*   **Faida:** Customer ko feedback liyera turunta change garna milcha. Flexible huncha.

**2. DevOps (Dev + Ops):**
*   **Concept:** Paila Developer (Code lekne) ra Operations (Server herne) bich jhagada hunthyo. DevOps le yiniharu lai **Combine** garcha.
*   **Goal:** Code lekhne bitikai production ma pathauna sakine (Automation).

**3. Cloud-Native & Microservices:**
*   **Monolithic:** Euta thulo software (change garna garo).
*   **Microservices:** Software lai sano-sano independent part ma tukraune (e.g., Login service xuttai, Payment xuttai). Euta bigryo vane aru lai asar gardaina.
*   **Cloud-Native:** Suru dekhi nai Cloud (AWS/Azure) ma chalne gari banaune.

**4. Data-Driven Decision Making:**
*   **Concept:** "Malai yesto lagcha" (Gut feeling) ko var ma decision naline. **Data ra Analytics** herera decision line.
*   **Example:** User le kun feature badi chalaudai chan? Tyo data herera next update plan garne.

**5. Cybersecurity Integration:**
*   **Concept:** Security vaneko last ma thapne kura haina. **Design phase** dekhi nai security sochnu parcha (**DevSecOps**).

**6. CI/CD (Continuous Integration / Continuous Delivery):**
*   **Concept:** Code change hune bitikai automatic test hune ra deploy hune pipeline. Manual kaam ghataune.

**7. Design Thinking:**
*   **Concept:** User ko problem lai **Empathy** (maaya) garera bujhne ani solution nikalne. "User lai k chaincha?" tyo center ma huncha.
