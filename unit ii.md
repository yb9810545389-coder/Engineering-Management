### **Unit II: Planning and Organizing (6 Hrs.)**

**Core Concept:**
Sir/Mam, Unit 1 ma hami le Management k ho vanera bujhyou. Aba Unit 2 ma hami **Kaam Kasari Suru Garne?** vanera sikchau.
*   **Planning** vaneko "Kaam suru garnu aghi **Sochnu** (Thinking before doing)."
*   **Organizing** vaneko "Socheko kura pura garna **Team ra Resource** milaunu."

---

### **2.1 Planning (Yojana)**

**1. Definition:**
Planning vaneko bholi k garne, kasari garne, kahile garne vanera aaja nai decide garne process ho.
*   *Neplish Logic:* "Fail to plan is planning to fail." Yadi tapai sanga map chaina vane, gन्तव्य (destination) ma pugna sakindaina.

**2. Levels of Planning (Hierarchy):**
Planning pani management level anusar farak huncha:
*   **Strategic Planning (Top Level):**
    *   *Time:* Long-term (5-10 years).
    *   *Focus:* "Company lai kaha puryaune?" (Vision & Mission).
    *   *Example:* "Ncell le Nepal ko sabai gaun ma 5G puryaune."
*   **Tactical Planning (Middle Level):**
    *   *Time:* Medium-term (1-2 years).
    *   *Focus:* "Strategic plan lai kasari execute garne?" (Departmental goals).
    *   *Example:* Network Department le "Yo barsa 500 wota tower thapne."
*   **Operational Planning (Lower Level):**
    *   *Time:* Short-term (Daily/Weekly).
    *   *Focus:* "Aaja k kaam garne?" (Routine tasks).
    *   *Example:* Technician le "Aaja tower number 105 ma gayera antenna fix garne."

**3. Steps in Planning Process (Viva Step-by-Step):**
Viva ma "Planning kasari garchau?" vanyo vane yo step vandinus:
1.  **Set Objectives:** Goal k ho? (Ex: Software banaune).
2.  **Develop Premises:** Assumption k chha? (Ex: Staff chhan, electricity chha).
3.  **Identify Alternatives:** K k tarika le garna sakincha? (Ex: Python ma garne ki Java ma?).
4.  **Evaluate & Select:** Kun chai best ho? (Ex: Python fast chha, tei choose garne).
5.  **Implement:** Kaam suru garne.
6.  **Review/Follow-up:** Plan anusar vayo ki vayena check garne.

**4. Tools for Planning:**
Plan hawa ma banaunalai hudaina, tools chaincha:
*   **SWOT Analysis:** Internal **S**trengths/ **W**eaknesses ra External **O**pportunities/ **T**hreats herne.
*   **Gantt Chart:** Project ko timeline dekhaune bar chart. (Kun kaam kahile sakine).
*   **P.E.S.T. Analysis:** **P**olitical, **E**conomic, **S**ocial, **T**echnological kura le plan lai k asar garcha?

**5. Importance of Planning:**
*   Uncertainty (Anischitata) ghataucha.
*   Direction dincha (Sabai jana eutai goal ma hidchan).
*   Waste ghataucha (Resource ko sahi use huncha).

### **2.2 Organizing (Sangathan/Sanrachana)**

**1. Definition:**
Plan banisakyo. Aba tyo plan pura garna **Structure** banaunu paryo. "Who will do what?" vanne kura Organizing ho.
*   *Process:* Kaam lai tukraune (Division of Work) $\to$ Department banaune (Departmentalization) $\to$ Power dine (Delegation).

**2. Organization Structure (Most Important Part):**
Company ko "Skeleton" (kankal) kasto huncha? Yaha bata question sure aauxa.

#### **A. Traditional Structures (Purano Style)**

**1. Line Organization:**
*   *Concept:* Military style. Order mathi bata sidha tala aauxa.
*   *Structure:* GM $\to$ Manager $\to$ Supervisor $\to$ Worker.
*   *Faida:* Decision chito huncha, Discipline huncha.
*   *Befaida:* Boss le je vanyo tei, Expert ko kami huncha.

**2. Functional Organization:**
*   *Concept:* Kaam ko aadhar ma department chuttyako.
*   *Structure:* IT Dept, HR Dept, Finance Dept, Marketing Dept.
*   *Faida:* Expert haru ek thau ma hunchan (Specialization).
*   *Befaida:* **"Silo Effect"** (IT wala le Marketing ko kura bujhdaina, communication gap huncha).

#### **B. Modern Structures (Naya Style - ICT ma use hune)**

**3. Matrix Organization (Viva Favorite):**
*   *Concept:* Yo **Hybrid** ho. Yesma employee ko **Dui Jana Boss** hunxan.
    *   **Functional Manager:** (Jastai Head of Coding - jasle talab, training hercha).
    *   **Project Manager:** (Jastai App Development Lead - jasle daily task hercha).
*   *Kina Engineering ma use huncha?* Resource share garna. Euta expert Database Engineer le bihana Project A ma ra diuso Project B ma kaam garna sakcha.
*   *Problem:* **Dual Command** (Dui jana boss vayesi confusion ra conflict huna sakcha).

**4. Network / Virtual Organization:**
*   *Concept:* "Core team" sano huncha, aru sabai kaam **Outsource** garincha.
*   *Example:* Euta Mobile company le Design aafai garcha, Manufacturing China ma, Call center India ma, Software Nepal ma.
*   *Faida:* Cost kam lagcha, flexible huncha.
*   *Befaida:* Control garna garo huncha.

**5. Hybrid Structure:**
*   *Concept:* Mathi ko sabai mix gareko. Thulo MNC (Google, Microsoft) ma use huncha.

### **2.4 Emerging Issues in ICT Enterprises**

Aaja ko IT company ma planning ra organizing garda k samasya aauxa?

1.  **Managing Rapid Tech Change:** Aaja banako plan 6 mahina pachi technology change vayera kaam nalagna sakcha. (Tei vayera Agile chaincha).
2.  **Remote Work (Virtual Teams):** Team members world ko farak farak time zone ma hunchan. Organize garna garo huncha.
3.  **Flat Structure:** Aaja kal "Boss" culture haraudai chha. Sabai sathi jasto vayera kaam garchan. Yeslai manage garna leadership skill chaincha.
4.  **Agility:** Market change hune bitikai Structure change garna sakne khalko huna parcha.
